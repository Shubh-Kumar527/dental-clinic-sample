About Us

Delhi's Most Trusted Dental Clinic

Smile Care Dental was founded in 2005 with a single mission: to provide world-class dental care that is accessible, compassionate, and result-driven. Located in South Delhi, we have grown from a two-chair clinic to a modern, 12-operatory facility serving thousands of patients across the city.

Our team of BDS and MDS-qualified dentists brings together decades of combined clinical experience, backed by continuous training in the latest techniques from India and abroad. We invest in state-of-the-art equipment — from digital X-rays to 3D CBCT scanners — so every treatment is precise, efficient, and comfortable.
